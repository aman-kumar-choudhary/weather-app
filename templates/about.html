About Polar Weather Forecast

Our Mission

Polar Weather Forecast provides accurate and up-to-date weather information for locations around the world. Our goal is to help you plan your days with reliable weather forecasts.

Features

  • Current weather conditions
  • 5-day weather forecast
  • Temperature, humidity, wind speed, and more
  • Global city coverage
  • Beautiful, intuitive interface

Technology

Our website is built using modern web technologies including Flask, Tailwind CSS, and the OpenWeatherMap API to deliver accurate weather data in an aesthetically pleasing format.
